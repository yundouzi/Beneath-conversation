The visualization shows the hidden thematic structure beneath conversational podcasts by combining topic categories, cross-categorized relationships, emotional tendencies, and temporal distributions into a single visual narrative. It reveals not only what topics are discussed and how long they occupy the conversation, but also how different themes connect with one another and evolve throughout an episode.

Introduction to the podcast- Yan Zhong Hua Shu

Yan Zhong Hua Shu is a long-form interview podcast hosted by Chen Luyu, featuring conversations with guests from diverse fields. Rich in personal narratives and multidisciplinary topics, it provides an ideal dataset for exploring thematic structures, emotional dynamics, and conversational patterns.

The title Yan Zhong Hua Shu (Flowers Growing from Rocks) symbolizes resilience and the power of overlooked voices, reflecting the podcast’s focus on revealing profound stories and ideas through conversation.
